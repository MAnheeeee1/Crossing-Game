1. A turtle moves forwards when you press the "Up" key.
It can only move forwards, not back, left or right.

2. Cars are randomly generated along the y-axis and will
move from the right edge of the screen to the left edge.

3. When the turtle hits the top edge of the screen,

 On the next level, the car speed increases.

4. When the turtle collides with a car,
it's game over and everything stops.
